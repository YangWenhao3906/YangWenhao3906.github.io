excerpt: "Agent instruction files (e.g., AGENTS.md, .cursorrules, SKILL.md) are increasingly used to guide AI coding assistants. We analyze 2,870 instruction-file pull requests across 2,246 repositories and find that over 70% receive no substantive review. We contribute a taxonomy of 16 review-concern categories and a benchmark showing current AI agents recover only 1.3-3.9% of the concerns humans raise."
